Don't Miss it



LIFE HAPPENS When life happens what do you do? Evey single person at one point or the other has been faced
with one chanllenge or the other, this could be as a result of hardship, disappointment, financial dififulties,
misfortune , heartbreak, etc.

Hence, having the wrong set of people in our lives can lead to making unhealthy decisions, rushing into
conculsion or rather after taken the right decision life still happens. Hmmmmm! Hereafter, this can happen to
anyone How do you cope with the challenges life throws at you?

do you just watch and and see it make a mess of you or you turn such mess to to a positive message to inspire
others? It is normal for you to feel disapointed and feel betrayed atfer trying your best only to dicover that
your best was not good enough. LIFE JUST HAPPENS!

To be a succes in life is a mtter of chocice, you have to be intentional about it and take a huge chuck of
responsiblities, you have to be responsible for your actions, interactions and reactions. You can make the choice
today to live an inspiring and fulfilling life that will make huge difference for others to emulate.
The choice is yours?

STEPS TO TAKE WHEN LIFE HAPPENS

Review your expectations: Take a closer look at the expectations you had before life happened. Do my
expectations set me up for disappointment? Try to learn from this experience it is not easy because you are
presently not happy with the situation all these will the change the course of our lives to yielding a better results.
After which you ask yourself how you could potentially adjust them for next time and what is one thing can do
differently the next time? Write them down and act on it immediately.

Take responsibility Acknowledge the present situation and move forward better days ahead. Identify your flaws
and amend them immediately. Please note that there is no time for pity party. Ann Landers once wrote, “Expect
trouble as an inevitable part of life, and when it comes, hold your head high. Look it squarely in the eye,and say,
“I will be bigger than you. You cannot defeat me.” For once in your life, the bigger you are, the better!

Allow cracks: A crack in your marriage, career, or personal plans doesn’t mean that your life is broken.
According to Canadian singer-songwriter Leonard Cohen, “There is a crack, a crack in everything. That’s how
the light gets in.” And one of the most powerful ways to do that is in my experience to let it out into the light and to
talk it over with someone close to you.


Hope against hope: There is one thing that never fails HOPE. You need to believe in yourself and stay hopeful.
Hope against hope, just believe it going to work out for good. Stay focus and positive.It is not easy but it is worthy
it and don’t be afraid to begin again. When you fall the seventh time get up and when you are tired just crawl.

Take a chill peel: Relax and enjoy yourself. So take some time to rest up, recharge and to have some fun. Just
focusing on your goals alone can cause unnecessary stress and anxiety. Sometimes you need to free your head
for a while after which you continue the work.

THERE IS SO MUCH HOPE

I am of the school thought that HARD WORK pays in the long run. You may not get what you want now but later,
with the requisite hard work and resilience, you will definitely get what you need.

Germany Kent is of the opinion that there is no shortcut for hard work that leads to effectiveness. You must stay
focused and disciplined because most of the work is done behind the scenes.”
According to Dido Stargaze, “It doesn't' matter how hard your life is, was, or will be, just don't give up”. So why
give up now when you are close to achieving your goals? Why bury yourself when you are still living?

Why throw- in the towel when you can still use it to dry your skin? Why being buried in frustration when there is
so much hope? Only if you can think, see and work it out in patience and perseverance. Good success is hard
work not for THE WEAK BUT THE WILLED.

Life is simple and can be compared to a GAME. What you need to do is play by the rules, and at the end, record a
resounding victory. The rules are usually not appealing, stressful and hard; but all you need is determination
and optimism at all cost for a better tomorrow. Be BOLD and take the right step because THERE IS SO MUCH
HOPE for a better tomorrow.

Don’t ever make the mistake of comparing yourself with others. You have a purpose and a destiny which you
MUST fulfil. So many people are attached to your destiny which you cannot afford to FAIL. The tougher the journey,
the closer to achieving your goals and fulfilling your purpose, SO DO NOT GIVE UP AND DO NOT FAIL YOUR
GENERATION.

CHALLENGES

Challenges are important parts of life that give you experiences. It make you learn and help you to become
wiser and stronger. Challenges in life can be used to your advantage. Each one is an opportunity for
personal growth and self-improvement. Ultimately, the goal is to use what you learn as you grow to become
the best version of yourself. Whether you lose your job, an opportunity, or a relationship, loss is an inevitable
part of life. Regardless of how it happens. Can I get a witness in the comment section?

YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR YOUR DESTINY

We all are indeed responsible for wherever we find ourselves. Our present state is a reflection of our past
actions, interactions and reactions to whatever life throws at us. Our decisions reflect our present state. If
you want to be successful, you have to take 100% responsibility for everything that you experience in life
which includes your achievements (relationships, health and income, your debts, emotions, career) etc. We all
have a mandate to fulfill here on earth, no one here is created without a purpose. The earlier we understand
this, the better.Hence, having this mindset we have to wake up to the call of RESPONSIBILITY.
This is not easy!

GEORGE WASHINGTON CARVER opines that Ninety-nine percent of all failures come from people who have
a habit of making excuses. Sometimes, some of us tend to shift blame outside of ourselves (We blame our
parents, bosses, friends, media, spouse and economy) for reasons we did not make through to achieving
our set goals. This mind set is totally wrong and must be IGNORED.

If you want to fulfill purpose here on earth, you have to take 100% responsibility for your actions, interactions
and reaction for whatever life throws at you. You must be willing to fight back and stay strong no matter how
difficult the situation is. You must be willing to turn your mess to a message of hope and adversity to advantage
if you truly want to fulfill purpose. It is not easy but worth it. Denzel Washington would have never become a
movie star, Bill Gates would never have founded Microsoft, and Steve Jobs would never have started Apple
Computers. For every reason why it is not possible, there are hundreds of people who have faced the same
circumstances and succeeded. So FACE IT.

Everything you EXPERIENCE today, is as a result of the CHOICES you have made in the past. You have to be
deliberate about your actions, Interactions and reactions about anything life throws in your path.

Actions:
There is a stimulus that prompts you to take a decision before an action is being taken, in that process you
have to be conscious of your mindset, filter out all negative thoughts and act positive to every situation.
Hence, having a deliberate mindset helps you to become cautious of every action you exhibit and teach you how
to sight wrong signals. Interaction: Interaction is a very virtual part of life. You need to get into the right
circle, communicate with people of like minds. Don’t be afraid to ask questions about your actions, most people
are afraid to ask for feedback about how they are doing because they are afraid of what the response might be
hmmm, I do this a lot but later take courage to get feedback. There is nothing to be afraid of. The truth is
the truth. You are better off knowing the truth than not knowing it, however, once you know, you can retract.
Interaction helps you to improve your life, your relationships, and your performances. Also, pay detailed
attention to feedback, this can mar or make you.

Reactions:
I believe that everyone has a purpose. Identifying, acknowledging, and honoring this purpose are perhaps the
most important action successful people take. They take the time to understand their purpose, stick with it and
pursue with enthusiasm. Without a purpose in life, it is easy to get distracted and achieve little in the journey
of life.

NOTE:YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR YOUR ACTIONS AND IT IS YOUR DUTY TO TAKE UP RESPONSIBILITY
FOR DESTINY OR MESS IT UP IN IGNORANCE. THE CHOICE IS LEFT FOR YOU TO DECIDE.
